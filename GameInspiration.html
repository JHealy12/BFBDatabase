Inspiration

One big inspiration to this games gameplay and theme is Sid Meiers Pirates. The movement of the boats and the battle mechanics are where the inspirations are really taken. Pirates was a game we felt a fun classic game that we could take many aspects from and put within our game to increase the enjoyment factor. The style of game will differ as Pirates is a more isometric game where as Black Flag Battles will be a top-down shooter. Another influence this game provides that isnt brought in from this game alone is the different ships that will be used. Black Flag Battle will incorporate different classes of ship different levels of ship will have different speeds, fire rates and different healths.

Another inspiration to Black Flag Battle is Assassins Creed 4 the ship battles in this game where revolutionary and entertaining. Another influence is Pirates of the Caribbean, this movies doesn't really influence game play but backs the theme choice as it is a family favourite.

Finally another major influence is our previous game Super Sonic Acrobatic Rocket Powered Battle Ship this games controls and game play. Black Flag Battles will take the same movement mechanics and game style as Super Sonic Acrobatic Rocket Powered Battle Ship. The games potential mixed with an effective theme such as pirates could be blended together to produce an extremely fun and effective game.
