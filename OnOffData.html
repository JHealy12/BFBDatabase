Online and Offline

The game has two modes available to play.

Online which will allow players to connect over the internet on separate networks or the same network, when playing online the users will have the opportunity to choose from three different class of ships; light, medium, and heavy. Each class will have different features from the other. Once the players have chosen their ships they will enter into the map and shall commence a battle to the death. each player has three lives and once they run out of lives their time upon the seas over.

The offline mode will allow two players to play on the same machine. The players will be able to choose different classes of ships and will enter into the map. Each player has three lives and once a player has used up all three of their lives their sailing days will be over and the player to be alive shall be known as the deadliest pirate on the seven seas.

Database

The database will be used to keep track of the offline wins and losses. This will allow for a more competitive offline battle. The database will count the wins and losses for player one and two, it will be purely offline to allow the competition of the same two players not to die out. The database will be simple in comparison to other databases but will be effective in use.
