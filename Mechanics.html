Mechanics

Physics

Since the game is top down it will be very limited to the physics incorporated. The physocs that will be in the game are movement of the ships. There will be a drag from the water resisting against the boat also when the boat stops propelling forward it will still float forward for a small time as it is floating on water. Another physics mechanic is the cannonballs will simulate gravity because after a while of being shot if they do not connect with anything they will disapear into the water.

Movement

The players will sail forward and rotate using the UP, Left and Right arrow keys or W,A,D. There will be some resistance on sailing and rotating will spin the boat as its still moving in the same direction.



Combat

The combat is real time. This will allow players to dodge each others attacks whilst counter attacking. Each player will have an allotted health depending on the ship when their health reaches zero the game will end with the survivor being the winner.
