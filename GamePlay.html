Controls

A and Left Arrow are to rotate player one and player two left.

W and Up Arrow are to sail for the players to sail forward.

D and Right Arrow are to rotate the players right.

S and Down Arrow are to shoot the cannons.


Battles

This game is a pirate themed battle game. Players will fight to the death. The world will be filled with objects that can cause damage so whilst fighting players have to sail wisly.
