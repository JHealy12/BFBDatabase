Game Objectives

Objective

The main objective will be to battle friends and other ships to the death. The last ship afloat will be the victor.

User Experience

The player should feel a competitive fun tactical battle. There should be a level of balance to the fights that allow the players a competitive fight with different classes of ship depending on the tactics.



Game Features

• Sea like sailing.

• Realistic shooting.

• Different ships to choose from.

• Array of different arenas.
