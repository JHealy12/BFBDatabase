Background Story

After the death of the captain the entire fleet couldn’t decide who to take charge this led to dissension in the ranks. The crew split into their own fleets and went their own ways looting and plundering the merchant and navy ships. After years the navy funded a new fleet that would come down on the pirates and make the seas safer. This forced the remaining fleets back together but still no leader could be decided so a tournament was created to put ship against ship to determine who captains the survivors and the fleet. This tournament is called the Black Flag Battles
